## 五、顶级SEO设置规范

### 5.1 Title Tag（标题标签）规范

```
格式：[主关键词] - [补充价值信息] | [品牌名]
字符限制：50-60字符（含空格）
```

**示例：**
- `Caustic Soda Flakes vs Pearls vs Liquid: Which to Choose | [Brand]`
- `Caustic Soda Price Guide 2025: Trends & Best Pricing | [Brand]`
- `Buy Caustic Soda from China: Complete Import Guide | [Brand]`

**规则：**
- 主关键词必须出现在标题前半部分
- 每个页面标题唯一，不重复
- 包含年份（适用于时效性内容）
- 使用数字和Power Words提高点击率

### 5.2 Meta Description（元描述）规范

```
字符限制：150-160字符
必须包含：主关键词 + 行动号召（CTA）
```

**示例：**
```
Learn the key differences between caustic soda flakes, pearls, and liquid forms. 
Find the right NaOH type for your application. Get expert advice from [Brand] — 
your trusted caustic soda supplier.
```

**规则：**
- 自然融入1-2个关键词
- 包含明确的价值主张
- 以CTA结尾（Get a quote, Contact us, Learn more）
- 每个页面元描述唯一

### 5.3 H标签层级规范

```
H1: 每页仅一个，包含主关键词（与Title可略有不同）
H2: 文章主要章节标题，包含辅助关键词或LSI关键词
H3: 子章节标题，包含长尾关键词变体
H4: 进一步细分（如需要）
```

**示例结构：**
```
H1: Caustic Soda Flakes vs Pearls vs Liquid: Complete Comparison Guide
  H2: What Are the Different Forms of Caustic Soda?
    H3: Caustic Soda Flakes: Properties and Characteristics
    H3: Caustic Soda Pearls: Properties and Characteristics
    H3: Liquid Caustic Soda (Lye): Properties and Characteristics
  H2: Key Differences Between Caustic Soda Flakes, Pearls, and Liquid
    H3: Physical Properties Comparison
    H3: Dissolution Rate and Ease of Use
    H3: Purity and Chemical Composition
  H2: Which Form of Caustic Soda Is Best for Your Application?
    H3: Best Caustic Soda for Water Treatment
    H3: Best Caustic Soda for Soap Making
    H3: Best Caustic Soda for Industrial Cleaning
  H2: Price Comparison: Flakes vs Pearls vs Liquid
  H2: How to Choose the Right Caustic Soda Form
  H2: FAQ: Caustic Soda Forms
```

### 5.4 URL 结构规范

```
格式：/blog/[primary-keyword-slug]
规则：
- 全小写
- 用连字符(-)分隔单词
- 不超过5-6个单词
- 包含主关键词
- 不含停止词（the, a, an, is, of 等可省略）
- 不含日期（除非是年度报告类）
```
