## Technical & Quality Questions

### 1. Why is Soda Ash Dense preferred for glass manufacturing?
Dense Soda Ash has a larger particle size and higher density, which prevents "batch segregation" in the furnace and reduces dust carry-over, protecting the furnace refractories.

### 2. Can I use Light Soda Ash for water treatment?
Yes. Light Soda Ash is often preferred for water treatment because its fine powder form allows for rapid dissolution in mixing tanks.

### 3. What is the impact of Chloride (Cl) in Soda Ash?
High chloride levels can cause stress corrosion in stainless steel equipment. For sensitive chemical synthesis, Hou process material (typically < 0.3% Cl) is preferred.

### 4. How do you measure "Total Alkali" content?
Total alkali is measured via potentiometric titration and expressed as the equivalent percentage of Na₂CO₃. Standard industrial grade is ≥ 99.2%.

### 5. What is the shelf life of Soda Ash?
In unopened, moisture-proof bags stored in a dry warehouse, Soda Ash has a shelf life of **24 months**.

## Logistics & Packaging Questions

### 6. What are the standard packaging options?
The most common are **50kg woven PP bags** and **1000kg/1250kg Jumbo bags (FIBCs)** with PE liners.

### 7. How do you prevent caking during sea transit?
We use high-micron (min. 100μm) PE liners, ensure the material is cool before bagging, and use strictly inspected, moisture-free containers.

### 8. Can you provide palletized shipments?
Yes. We offer heat-treated (ISPM-15) wooden pallets or plastic pallets, typically loading 20-24 Jumbo bags per 20'GP.

### 9. What is the lead time from a China port?
Typically **7-14 days** from order confirmation to port loading, depending on the current vessel schedule and container availability.

### 10. Which China ports are best for Soda Ash export?
**Tianjin (Xingang)** and **Lianyungang** are the primary hubs, offering the most frequent sailings and specialized chemical loading facilities.

## Sourcing & Compliance Questions

### 11. How do I verify a factory's ISO certification?
You can verify the certificate number on the **CNAS** website or the specific international registrar's database (e.g., SGS or TUV).

### 12. Is a Pre-Shipment Inspection (PSI) recommended?
For new suppliers or high-volume contracts, we highly recommend a PSI by **SGS or Intertek** to verify quality and quantity before loading.

### 13. What is the role of Sinosure in my procurement?
Sinosure provides the credit insurance that allows us to offer you flexible payment terms like **OA 30-90 days**.

### 14. Are there any export taxes on Soda Ash from China?
Currently, there are no specific export taxes, but VAT rebate policies can change, influencing the final FOB price.

### 15. How do I handle a quality dispute?
We maintain retention samples for every batch. If a dispute arises, we use a mutually agreed-upon third-party lab for independent testing.

## Strategic & Market Questions

### 16. What drives the price of Soda Ash in China?
The primary drivers are **energy costs (coal/gas)**, factory maintenance schedules, and global supply-demand balance.

### 17. When is the best time to buy Soda Ash?
Avoid the peak maintenance season (July-August) and the winter energy peak. Securing Q2 and Q4 volumes in advance is often the most cost-effective strategy.

### 18. Is "Natural" Soda Ash better than "Synthetic"?
Natural Soda Ash often has lower impurities (Fe, Cl), but synthetic (Solvay/Hou) offers higher volume stability and more consistent PSD for large-scale industrial use.

### 19. How is the "Green Ammonia" trend affecting Soda Ash?
The industry is moving toward "Green Soda Ash" by using renewable energy and green ammonia in the Hou process to reduce the carbon footprint.
