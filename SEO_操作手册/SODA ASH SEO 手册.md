## 2. World-Class Writing Standards (E-E-A-T & B2B UX)

### A. The "30-Second Rule" (For Scanning Buyers)
*   **Mandatory Summary Table**: Every post MUST start with a table (e.g., Specs, Price Trends, or Grade Comparisons).
*   **F-Pattern Layout**: Use H2/H3 subheadings every 300 words. Bold key high-intent terms.
*   **No "Fluff"**: Avoid generic phrases like "We are high quality." Use: "Purity ≥ 99.2%, Fe ≤ 0.003%."
