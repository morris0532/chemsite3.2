## 1. Quality Assurance and Certification
The foundation of any chemical partnership is consistent, high-quality material.
*   **ISO 9001 Certification**: Does the supplier maintain a robust Quality Management System?
*   **Certificate of Analysis (COA)**: Can they provide batch-specific COAs that include critical parameters like B₂O₃ content, iron levels, and mesh size?
*   **Traceability**: Can the supplier trace each batch back to the original mine and refinery?
*   **Compliance**: For specific industries, do they meet **REACH (EU)**, **RoHS**, or **Food/Pharma grade** standards?

---

## 2. Supply Chain Reliability and Capacity
A supplier is only as good as their ability to deliver when you need it most.
*   **Inventory Levels**: Do they maintain local or regional safety stocks to buffer against global shipping delays?
*   **Lead Times**: What is their standard lead time for FCL (Full Container Load) vs. LCL shipments?
*   **Logistics Network**: Do they have established relationships with major shipping lines and inland freight providers?
*   **Financial Stability**: Does the supplier have the financial strength to weather market volatility and invest in long-term supply security?

---

## 3. Technical Expertise and Support
In the B2B world, you often need more than just a product; you need a solution.
*   **Application Knowledge**: Does the supplier understand the specific requirements of your industry (e.g., glass, agriculture, or detergents)?
*   **R&D Capabilities**: Can they help you optimize your formulation or suggest the most cost-effective grade of Borax for your process?
*   **Regulatory Support**: Can they provide up-to-date MSDS/SDS and assist with complex regulatory filings?

---

## 4. Transparency and Communication
Effective partnerships are built on clear, honest communication.
*   **Market Intelligence**: Does the supplier provide regular updates on global market trends, energy prices, and potential supply disruptions?
*   **Order Tracking**: Do they provide real-time tracking and proactive notifications regarding shipment status?
*   **Responsiveness**: How quickly does their sales and technical team respond to inquiries or quality concerns?

---

## 5. Sustainability and Ethics
Modern procurement requires a commitment to responsible sourcing.
*   **Environmental Policy**: Does the supplier have a clear policy on minimizing their environmental footprint?
*   **Ethical Sourcing**: Do they ensure that their supply chain is free from forced labor and adheres to international human rights standards?
